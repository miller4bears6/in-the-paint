Galileo Galilei - The Father of Modern Science

Today, we are going to explore some of the great achievements of Galileo Galilei. He contributed to physics, cosmology, mathematics, philosophy, and astronomy.

Galileo believed in research and scientific methods and this clashed with The Church. The Church held the belief that God created Earth as the center of the universe making it powerful in the grand scheme of things.
