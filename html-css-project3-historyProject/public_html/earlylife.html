Galileo was born in Pisa, Italy in 1564. He started his education on the medical track and soon changed his major to mathematics. His family was quite unsupportive. He actually did not finish college. His first job was tutoring math students. Interesting!

Greek mythologies captivated his interested and he began to study them. Galileo published his first book, The Little Balance, which was an extension of Archimedes' work.

During the early years of studying great philosophers, Gallileo began an interest in testing principles. Today, we know those principles as The Scientific Method.
